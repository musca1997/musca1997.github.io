LAST BLOOD FOR MUSE

adorned lucien, kindly takes away your life
deconstructs your inmost fears and old deals
and misters, they called me to rise at dawn
extremely loud sound of wishes left me hung
them the tears, i could be the one to blame
give all my understandings to weak ghost's flame

the time has come, what used to shout in my head
appear in the cloth of colors, like usual surroundings
after a painless struggle you'll be the jewel, for the glory
of your loved ones' death, somehow i kept my mouth shut
now stand, see them pass by, at this threaten night
rebuild your hardened mind and i will take away the might
behind those which store the dissatisfaction of spirits
how can we hide heartlessly behind our rigorous words?
